The idea

Not another kids class. A club for real-world childhood.

Offscreen Kids Club brings parents and kids together for small, curated weekend drops: skate, scooter, roller, football, biking, hiking, ski days and other skills kids remember because they learned them with someone they love.

How it works

Simple enough to launch from a Facebook post.

01

Pick a drop

Parents join small weekend activities by age, skill and location.

02

Show up together

Dads actively participate. Kids learn by doing, not watching.

03

Leave with a memory

Each drop ends with a photo, badge or small ritual kids can remember.

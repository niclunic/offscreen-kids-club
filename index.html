What it is

A club for offline childhood.

How it works

A simple system for better weekends.

Offscreen Kids Club is designed to feel easy for parents and reliable for kids: curated drops, clear capacity, parent participation and memories after every session.

01

Choose a drop

Pick a small session by age, skill, location and vibe. Every drop shows capacity, gear and level.

02

03

Build the memory

Kids leave with a small win, a badge or memory pack, and a reason to come back next weekend.

Safety and trust

Small groups. Clear gear. Parents present.

The goal is not to create another chaotic kids plan. Every drop should feel clear before families arrive.

Parent-present

Parents or guardians stay present and remain responsible during the activity.

Not childcare

OKC organizes family activity drops. It is not a drop-off childcare service.

Clear capacity

Every drop has an approved group size based on age, skill and activity risk.

Required gear

Every drop lists whether gear is required, optional, provided or brought by families.

Helmets for wheels

Helmets are required for wheels-based drops like skate, scooter and rollers.

Higher-risk standards

Ski, snow, water, climbing or similar drops use stricter standards or professional partners.

Captain Parents

Have a skill? Turn it into OKC credits.

Captain Parents host small, approved drops around skills they love. They set the maximum group size they feel comfortable managing and define the required gear upfront: helmets, rollers, instruments, tools, no gear, or gear they can bring for everyone. In return, Captains earn OKC credits to use on other activities, plus status, merch and priority access.

FAQ

Questions parents usually ask.

Is this childcare?

No. A parent or guardian stays present and remains responsible during the activity.

Can I join without becoming a member?

Yes. Selected drops can be booked separately when spots are available.

What does Founding Parents include?

One monthly family drop, priority access, member pricing for extra drops, private Prague chapter and memory packs.

Can mothers, single parents or divorced parents join?

Yes. The club is open to parents and guardians. The goal is better real-world memories with kids.

Can I host something?

Yes. Apply as a Captain Parent. Tell us your skill, max comfortable group size and required gear.

Does my child need experience?

No. Most first drops are beginner-friendly and grouped by age, skill and confidence.

Is gear included?

It depends on the drop. Every drop clearly lists whether gear is required, optional, provided or brought by families.

What if it rains?

Weather-sensitive drops are rescheduled or credited.

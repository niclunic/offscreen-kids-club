# Drop Pricing Standard

## Authoritative launch rule

This document supersedes any earlier wording that described the founding price as “per family.”

At launch, Offscreen Kids Club prices Drops **per participating child** because capacity, gear, risk and Captain ratios are calculated by the number of children.

### Standard display

> From 490 CZK per child. One attending parent or guardian included.

## Booking unit

One booking place includes:

- One participating child
- One attending parent or guardian
- The activity and Captain-led experience
- Any materials explicitly listed as included

## Siblings

- Every additional participating child requires a separate place unless the Drop explicitly states otherwise.
- Sibling discounts may be tested on lower-cost or high-capacity Drops.
- Sibling rules must be shown before payment.

## Price categories

### Standard founding Drop

Target starting point: approximately **490 CZK per child**.

Examples:

- First Wheels
- First Sounds
- Build Club
- First Trails
- Photo Walks

### Lower-complexity community Drop

May test below the standard starting point when venue and material costs are minimal.

Example range: 390 to 490 CZK per child.

### Material or specialist Drop

Higher price when the Drop includes substantial materials, specialist instruction, equipment or venue cost.

Example range: 590 to 790 CZK per child.

### Premium seasonal Drop

Priced separately based on professional instruction, transport, equipment, insurance, venue and duration.

Examples:

- First Tracks
- Water activities
- Climbing
- Mountain biking

## Price communication rule

Every Drop must show before booking:

- Exact price per child
- What is included
- Whether one parent or guardian is included
- Sibling policy
- Gear costs or rental requirements
- Refund, cancellation and weather-credit policy

## Waitlist

Joining the founding waitlist is free.

No card details, recurring billing or payment should be requested until the family actively chooses a specific Drop.

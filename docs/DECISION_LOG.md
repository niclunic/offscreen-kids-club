# Offscreen Kids Club Decision Log

## 2026-07-22: Launch model

**Decision:** launch with pay-per-Drop only.

- No monthly subscription during initial validation
- Founding waitlist is free
- Families pay only when booking a specific Drop
- Founding standard Drops begin from approximately 490 CZK per family
- Exact price, capacity, age, level, gear and cancellation terms are shown before booking
- Premium seasonal or specialist Drops are priced separately

**Reason:** a concrete Drop is easier to understand and purchase than an abstract membership before the product has been experienced. Recurring membership will be reconsidered only after repeat demand and calendar reliability are proven.

## 2026-07-22: Market positioning

**Decision:** parent-first brand with a dad-origin story.

- Started through Dads in Prague
- Open to mums, dads, guardians, sons and daughters
- Captain Parent is the universal host category
- The brand remains premium, cool, skill-based and parent-present

**Reason:** dads provide a strong launch beachhead, while the broader parent market provides frequency, weekday demand, diverse Captains and a larger commercial opportunity.

## 2026-07-22: Product definition

**Decision:** the unit of product is a Drop.

Every Drop must define:

- Objective
- Age
- Level
- Capacity
- Duration
- Captain
- Parent role
- Gear
- Price
- Safety standard
- Weather and cancellation terms

**Reason:** repeatability and trust require activities to be designed as products rather than informal gatherings.

## 2026-07-22: Captain incentive

**Decision:** begin with OKC credits, status and community benefits, with a pathway to formal payment for reliable repeat Captains.

**Reason:** credits create internal circulation and community contribution during the pilot, but professional or recurring work must eventually be compensated fairly and formally.

## 2026-07-22: Operating model

**Decision:** Offscreen remains a managed system during validation.

Offscreen approves:

- Captain
- Playbook
- Capacity
- Age band
- Gear
- Venue
- Price
- Communication

**Reason:** an open marketplace would create inconsistent quality, safety risk and a cold-start problem.

## 2026-07-22: Long-term company thesis

**Decision:** build the operating system for offline childhood.

Long-term assets:

- Brand
- Drop Playbooks
- Captain network
- Chapter system
- Booking and family data
- Safety and quality standards
- B2B distribution
- Partnerships and merchandise

**Reason:** founder wealth is expected to come from equity in a repeatable international system, not only from margin on individual Prague activities.

## Subscription reconsideration gate

A recurring membership may be tested only after:

- 50 paying families
- 6 completed paid Drops
- 3 repeatable Drop formats
- 35% second-Drop attendance within 90 days
- 4 reliable Captains
- Predictable monthly calendar

Until then, the commercial model remains pay per Drop.

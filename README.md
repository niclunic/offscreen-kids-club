# Offscreen Kids Club

**Less screen time. More first times.**

Offscreen Kids Club is a parent-present offline activity club for mums, dads, guardians and kids. Prague is the first operating laboratory.

## Current launch model

- No monthly subscription at launch
- Families join the founding waitlist for free
- Every Drop is booked and paid separately
- Founding standard Drops are expected to start from 490 CZK per family
- Every Drop shows price, date, age, capacity, level, gear and cancellation terms before booking
- Captain Parents can host approved Drops and earn OKC credits
- The product is parent-present and is not childcare

## Strategic objective

Build a repeatable operating system for offline childhood:

1. Validate paid Drops in Prague
2. Standardize successful formats into Drop Playbooks
3. Build a trusted Captain Parent network
4. Remove the founder from day-to-day Drop execution
5. Prove a second city chapter
6. Expand through chapters, B2B family benefits, partnerships and brand products

## Live product

- Static landing page deployed through Vercel
- Lead capture through Formspree
- Mobile-first conversion layer
- Inclusive positioning for mums, dads, guardians, sons and daughters
- Pay-per-Drop launch offer

## Launch objective

Use Dads in Prague as the first acquisition channel, while keeping the club open to all parents and guardians. The goal is not to collect vanity signups. The goal is to fill paid Drops, create repeat attendance, identify strong Captains and document a system that works without depending entirely on Nico.
